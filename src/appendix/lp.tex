Primal:
\begin{align*}
\max \quad & z = 2x_1 + x_2 + 3x_3 + x_4 \\
\text{s.t.} \quad & x_1 + x_2 + x_3 + x_4 \leq 5 \\
& 2x_1 - x_2 + 3x_3 = -4 \\
& x_1 - x_3 + x_4 \geq 1 \\
& x_1, x_3 \geq 0
\end{align*}

Dual:
\begin{align*}
\min \quad & w = 5y_1 - 4y_2 + y_3 \\
\text{s.t.} \quad & y_1 + 2y_2 + y_3 \geq 2 \\
& y_1 - y_2 = 1 \\
& y_1 + 3y_2 - y_3 \geq 3 \\
& y_1 + y_3  = 1 \\
& y_1 \geq 0, y_3 \leq 0
\end{align*}
